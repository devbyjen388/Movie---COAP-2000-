Us And Them

Rene Liu

I cannot remember when I first watched this movie. But I remember having strong feelings about XiaoXiao and Jian Qing's split and the bitterness of their ending.

This movie makes me think about the what ifs. What if I listened to mom and kept my room tidy would I have become a professional cleaner? Probably not.

While they were getting used to eachother everything felt really strong, saying hello got them excited looking at eachother got them excited. It reminds me of yesterday when I opened a Hirono blind box, the excitement of getting the one I wanted now I do not even think about it.

Good things are often situational. Even when they are good, there are times when we fail to recognize them.

"Happiness is not a story, misfortune is."

— Fang Yuan
