About Me

Hello, my name is Jennifer Dinh. This site was created with the intention of sharing my favorite movies. I consider myself an uncultured person, such that I've never watched any Marvel movies before. Other than that, I know what movies can do to people. For me they act as a bridge that allows me to travel to another country with a low price tag. Whenever I want to see Vietnam, Switzerland, or HongKong I can just rent out a movie or borrow my cousin's Netlflix subscription. Places, people, culture, and mood are factors that draw me into a movie, my favorites are not just watched once but multiple times.

Sometimes I wish there was a movie created for the best experiences in my life so that I would never forget them.

I do not remember the last movie I have watched. Now that I have more responsibilites the time I spend watching movies decrease, I hope life is not always like this and that movie time is plentiful.
