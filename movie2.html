Parasite

Bong Joon Ho

One of my best friends during highschool recommended me this movie. A poor family of 4 social engineers their way into a wealthy family, they gain a lot in the moment but lose much more in the end.

This movie is about greed, status, and connection.

Whenever I watch this movie I think about all the times I have pretended to be someone I am not and the difficulty of accepting your circumstances, who you are, and what you smell like.

In this movie the wealth are indifferent and the poor greedy. When I say poor in these paragraphs I am talking poor in money. The poor family were wealthy in a way that they had each other.

"Money is an iron, it smoothes out all the creases"
